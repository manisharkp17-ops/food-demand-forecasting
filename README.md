# Food Demand Forecasting System

## Project Overview

The Food Demand Forecasting System is a Machine Learning project that predicts future food demand based on factors such as price, promotions, and holidays. Accurate demand forecasting helps restaurants, supermarkets, and food delivery businesses manage inventory efficiently, reduce food wastage, and improve customer satisfaction.

## Problem Statement

Food businesses often face challenges in estimating future demand accurately. Underestimating demand can lead to stock shortages and dissatisfied customers, while overestimating demand results in excess inventory and food wastage. Therefore, an intelligent forecasting system is required to predict food demand effectively.

## Objectives

* Predict future food demand using historical data.
* Reduce food wastage and operational costs.
* Improve inventory and stock management.
* Support data-driven business decisions.
* Increase customer satisfaction through better availability of food items.

## Technologies Used

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Scikit-learn

## Machine Learning Algorithm

* Linear Regression

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains the following attributes:

* Price – Cost of the food item.
* Promotion – Whether a promotional offer is active (0 = No, 1 = Yes).
* Holiday – Whether the day is a holiday (0 = No, 1 = Yes).
* Demand – Number of food items demanded.

## Methodology

1. Generate or collect food demand data.
2. Perform data preprocessing and analysis.
3. Split the dataset into training and testing sets.
4. Train the Linear Regression model.
5. Predict future food demand.
6. Evaluate model performance using MAE and R² Score.
7. Visualize the results using graphs.

## Expected Output

The system predicts future food demand based on input values such as price, promotion status, and holiday information. The prediction helps businesses plan inventory and production more effectively.

## Benefits

* Reduces food wastage.
* Improves inventory planning.
* Enhances operational efficiency.
* Increases profitability.
* Supports informed decision-making.

## Future Enhancements

* Use larger real-world datasets.
* Implement advanced algorithms such as Random Forest and XGBoost.
* Develop a web-based dashboard for real-time forecasting.
* Integrate sales and weather data for improved accuracy.

## Conclusion

The Food Demand Forecasting System demonstrates how Machine Learning can be used to predict future food demand accurately. By leveraging historical data and predictive analytics, businesses can optimize inventory management, reduce waste, and improve overall efficiency.
